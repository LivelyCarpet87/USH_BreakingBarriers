Thesis

The Apple company broke the barrier of technical complexity that prevented the general public from using computers for everyday tasks. Computers before the Apple II required complex understanding and knowledge to use. The Apple II made computers more accessible to the general public by implementing a Graphical User Interface and pre-installed programs. This formed a new industry standard and broke down the barrier of complexity barring the computer from being used in everyday life. Before Apple released the iPhone, phones were limited to only calling and texting due to its small screen and limited keyboard. The introduction of the iPhone made it possible to integrate more technologically advanced features into phones that could be used intuitively.
