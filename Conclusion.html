Conclusion

Apple was able to shatter the barrier of technical complexity barring the general public from becoming computer users with the Apple II. It did so by setting new industry standards after its launch, such as:

  • running out of the box

  • implementing Graphical User Interfaces

Apple was able to shatter the barrier of technical complexity and allowing people to become owners of smartphones with the iPhone2G. It did so by setting new industry standards after its launch, such as:

  • Touchscreen Display

  • Increased Memory Size

These changes made technology easier to use and understand, leading eventually to life today.
